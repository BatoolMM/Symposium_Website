The Global Dynamics in

A Two-day Virtual Symposium on December 1–2

About The Event

About The Symposium

This symposium is organised by The Einstein Foundation with eLife Community Ambassadors. It will dedicated to early career researchers who will define the future research culture and incentives that drive researchers’ behaviours.

Where

Online Symposium followed by Einstein Foundation Award onsite in Berlin.

When

Thursday to Friday
1-2 December 2022.

The Organising Team

The Symposium is conceived and organized by Einstein Foundation and an international team of eLife Community Ambassadors, the event will provide a platform for the perspectives of early career researchers on current challenges

The Einstein Foundation Berlin is an independent, not-for-profit, science-led organization established as a foundation under civil law. For over ten years, it has promoted international cutting-edge science and research across disciplines and institutions in and for Berlin. It has funded almost 200 researchers, including three Nobel laureates, over 70 projects, and seven Einstein Centers.
